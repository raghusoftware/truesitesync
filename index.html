Frequently asked questions

What is construction management software?

A digital platform that replaces measurement books, Excel billing sheets and paper registers with one system covering site measurement, BOQ-linked billing, inventory, labour and project cash flow — so you always know real profit per project.

Is True Site Sync a construction ERP?

Yes — measurement, BOQ, RA billing, inventory, labour, planning and accounts in one system. Unlike enterprise construction ERPs built for large multi-site organisations, it's priced and scoped for solo engineers and small-to-mid contractors, starting at ₹2,500/year with a self-serve 7-day free trial — no sales demo required.

How much does construction management software cost in India?

Enterprise construction ERPs often require a sales demo before you see pricing. True Site Sync publishes it upfront: ₹2,500/year solo, scaling to ₹50,000/year for up to 50 users, every module included at every tier.

Does it work offline on site?

Yes. Entries save on the device immediately and sync to the cloud automatically once connected — built for engineers at remote sites with patchy internet.

Can my whole team use it together?

Yes — every plan above Solo supports multiple users with role-based permissions (Admin, Accountant, Site Supervisor, Engineer), sharing one live, org-wide dataset with real-time sync.

How is this different from a traditional construction ERP?

Traditional construction ERPs are built for large enterprises with dedicated IT teams and a sales-demo onboarding process.
