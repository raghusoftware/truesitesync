Why a digital measurement sheet

The site engineer measures, writes it in a paper book, and someone later re-types it into Excel to make a bill. Every hand-off loses time and adds mistakes — and when a measurement is questioned, the original record is hard to find. A digital measurement sheet captures the quantity once, at source, and keeps it linked to every bill that uses it.

Measurement sheet software — FAQs

What is measurement sheet software?

It replaces the paper measurement book — you record dimensions or direct quantities digitally, and the software totals them and links them to billing items.

Does it work offline on site?

Yes — it is offline-first. Record measurements without internet and they sync to the cloud and your team automatically.

Can I convert measurements into a bill?

Yes — convert a sheet into an abstract, RA bill, tax invoice or estimate in one click, carrying quantities and rates through.

Can I record kg / MT quantities?

Yes — enter L×B×H or a direct quantity in any unit, and items are grouped and totalled correctly.
