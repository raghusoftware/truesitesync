The problem with RA billing in Excel

Most contractors prepare RA bills in spreadsheets — copying quantities from the measurement book, manually subtracting the previous bill, looking up BOQ rates, and re-calculating retention every cycle. One wrong cell and the bill is short-paid or rejected by the client. As projects grow, this becomes hours of error-prone work every month.

True Site Sync connects measurement → abstract → RA bill into one chain, so every figure is calculated for you and traceable back to the site record.

RA bill software — FAQs

What is RA bill software?

RA bill software automates Running Account billing — it tracks work done up to date, subtracts what was already billed, applies BOQ rates, deducts retention, and produces a GST invoice.

Can I make an RA bill directly from a measurement sheet?

Yes. Record measurements, generate the abstract, and convert it to an RA bill in one click. Quantities and rates flow through automatically.

Does it handle retention and previous RA deductions?

Yes — every RA bill shows up-to-date quantity, previous-bill quantity and the current payable difference, and applies your retention percentage.
