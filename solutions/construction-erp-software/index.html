What "construction ERP software" actually means

A construction ERP connects the parts of running a site that normally live in separate places — the measurement book, the BOQ, the billing spreadsheet, the material register, the labour muster — into one system where they reconcile automatically. Search for "construction ERP software" in India and you'll mostly find large enterprise platforms built for multi-site, multi-country organisations with dedicated implementation teams. True Site Sync is the same category of software — a genuine construction ERP — built and priced for the much larger group of small and mid-size contractors those enterprise tools were never designed for.

Enterprise construction ERPs vs a self-serve construction ERP

Established construction ERP vendors in India and globally — the category includes names like Xpedeon, Tactive and NWAY — are mature, capable platforms with large enterprise client bases, ISO certifications, and features suited to organisations running dozens of concurrent projects. Their published sites emphasise enterprise trust signals: tens of thousands of users, ISO 27001/SOC2 certifications, and a "book a demo" onboarding flow, because their pricing and implementation are typically quoted per organisation.

True Site Sync covers the same core discipline — measurement-to-billing, BOQ tracking, inventory, labour, accounts — but takes a different path to get there: publicly listed pricing, a self-serve sign-up, and a 7-day free trial you start yourself, today, without a sales call.
