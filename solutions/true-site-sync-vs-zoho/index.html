True Site Sync vs Zoho: which fits a construction site?

Zoho is a broad business suite built for many industries. True Site Sync is built for one thing: measurement-to-RA-bill construction billing for Indian contractors. Here's the honest comparison.

See True Site Sync pricingTry free

The short answer

If your business is broader than construction — you also run services, retail, or need a generic CRM/HR/finance stack — Zoho's suite of 45+ apps genuinely has more breadth. But if what you actually need is digital measurement sheets, BOQ-linked quantities, and RA (Running Account) bills with retention and GST raised the way Indian contractors actually raise them, that workflow doesn't exist natively in Zoho. You'd be assembling it yourself across Zoho Projects, Zoho Books and spreadsheets. True Site Sync does it in one app, out of the box.

What Zoho is

Zoho Projects is a general project-management tool — Gantt charts, task dependencies, milestones, timesheets. Zoho Books and Zoho Invoice handle accounting and generic invoicing, and can convert logged timesheet hours into an invoice. None of these three apps have a concept of a BOQ item, an executed quantity against a contract, a previous-bill quantity, or retention — the specific mechanics of construction RA billing. Construction firms using Zoho typically license and connect two or three separate apps, then build the billing logic themselves in a spreadsheet alongside it.

What True Site Sync is

One app, built around the site-to-bill cycle: record measurements against BOQ items (Nos × L × B × H), generate the abstract at agreed rates, raise the RA bill with previous/current/balance quantity, retention and GST applied automatically, track material consumption against the same BOQ, run labour attendance and wages, and see real profit per project on a cash-flow dashboard — without connecting or configuring separate apps.

True Site Sync vs Zoho — frequently asked questions

Is Zoho good for construction companies?

It's a capable general suite, but has no native BOQ, measurement sheet, or RA bill workflow — you'd configure multiple apps to approximate it.

What does True Site Sync do that Zoho doesn't?

Native digital measurement sheets, BOQ-linked quantities, RA bills with retention/GST, BOQ-tied inventory, and a per-site cash-flow view — in one app.

Is True Site Sync cheaper than Zoho?
